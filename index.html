About

During the Middle Ages, as legend would have it, a mysterious Black Knight appeared – fighting to protect the rights of the downtrodden. For his good deeds he was granted immortality and charged with keeping the universal balance between good and evil. According to the myth he roams the world today shrouded in a cloak of anonymity doing good works as the Black Knight.

The story of the Black Knight’s struggle and subsequent triumph over evil inspired the creation of Black Knight Music. A collection of powerful and emotional epic music adding dynamic impact to Film Trailers and TV Promos. A fitting soundtrack to the continuing challenge of overcoming adversity in today’s world.
